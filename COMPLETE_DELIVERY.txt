💬 COMMON QUESTIONS:
─────────────────────

Q: How do I change the port from 5000?
A: Edit app_ui.py, last line, change 5000 to desired port

Q: How do I clear all data?
A: Control Panel → Database → Clear Database (need password)

Q: Where is my data stored?
A: In parking.db SQLite database (auto-created)

Q: Can I use this on a network?
A: Yes! Just use server IP instead of localhost

Q: How do I export data?
A: Reports tab → Export CSV button

Q: How do I change billing rates?
A: Edit billing.py configuration at top

Q: Can I integrate cameras?
A: Yes! Integrate with parking_system.py
